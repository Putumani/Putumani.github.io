About Me

Hi, I’m Putumani Nomzaza, a passionate software and web developer weaving creativity into code. My tech journey began during my Electrical Engineering studies at Cape Peninsula University of Technology, where a programming module ignited my love for solving problems through code.

At WeThinkCode_, I built a responsive tutorial website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, guiding 100+ new students through tools like Slack, LMS, and Google Spaces. As a WeThinkCode_ and Umuzi alumnus, I earned NQF Level 5 qualifications in Software and Web Development.

I also served as a Web Development Recruit at Umuzi, mentoring aspiring developers, and now freelance as a Manual QA Tester at test.io, ensuring app quality by hunting bugs. I’m skilled in Java, Python, JavaScript, Node.js, and Flutter, thriving in Agile environments.

When I’m not coding, I’m diving into YouTube tutorials, watching movies, or staying inspired by fashion and tech trends. My drive for problem-solving keeps me engaged, and I’m excited to create innovative solutions.
